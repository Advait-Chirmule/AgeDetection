Introduction



This is a fun project in which you can check out your age and whether or not you fall in the age range you desire! Follow these simple steps:
a) Go to the "Prediction" tab
b) There is an option to upload an image, click on it and upload an image which contains the face you want to predict the age of. Remember, this model predicts based on the face so make sure the front face is visible!
c) Click on Enter. Also, remember this needs Internet so even if you have downloaded the files you would still need the Internet!
d) And that's it! You shall get your predicted age soon!
